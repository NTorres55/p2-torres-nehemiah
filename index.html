What We Offer


The mission of the North Carolina Society of Hispanic Professionals (NCSHP) is to promote the education of Hispanic students at all scholastic levels. We are a professional membership with a greater mission, supporting Hispanic students in their academic pursuits and enabling them to achieve their dreams!

Through educational programs and initiatives, we INSPIRE students to stay in school ENGAGE Hispanic families in the education of their children MAKE DREAMS COME TRUE by helping students to graduate from High School and go to College! We believe that an investment in a Hispanic student is an investment in North Carolina.
